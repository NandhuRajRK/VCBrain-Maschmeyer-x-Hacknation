# Iskra Pitch Script

## 0:00 - The Problem

“A venture team can read a pitch deck in minutes, but a trustworthy investment
decision still takes much longer. Claims are scattered across decks, public
signals, founder history, spreadsheets, CRM notes, and partner conversations.
The dangerous part is not slow summarization. It is losing the evidence, missing
a contradiction, or treating a founder's lack of public data as a negative.”

## 0:30 - The Product

“Iskra is an AI-first VC operating system for a decision on a $100,000 check
within 24 hours. It turns every document and signal into a traceable record of
sources, claims, evidence, confidence, and gaps. The intelligence layer then
produces a thesis fit, three independent scores, a memo, and a clear next action.”

## 1:00 - Start With The Portfolio

Open the dashboard.

“This is the investor cockpit. It answers four questions immediately: what is
active, what is decision-ready, what diligence is still open, and where is the
portfolio geographically concentrated? The global intelligence view is
interactive, so I can switch between companies and founders, filter geography,
and open a specific profile.”

## 1:45 - Source Before Scoring

Open `/sourcing` or Deal Flow and select AetherGrid.

“AetherGrid starts with a pitch deck, founder history, customer reference,
market note, financials, and public signals. Iskra normalizes those inputs into
one evidence model. A lead is not treated as a company until the investor
reviews its identity, source, confidence, and thesis relevance.”

## 2:20 - The Decision Workspace

Open the company review and show Decision, Analysis, Founder Passports, and
Evidence.

“The Decision tab gives the readiness score and the next actions. Analysis keeps
Founder, Market, and Idea-vs-Market separate; these axes are never hidden inside
one average. The Founder Passport preserves employment, education, previous
ventures, skills, source IDs, confidence, and explicit gaps. Evidence shows the
claim, its status, source, freshness, independence, and confidence reason.”

## 3:00 - The Wow Moment: Contradictions

Ingest the staged independent correction for AetherGrid.

“The deck says twenty enterprise customers. The new independent signal says the
company has five pilots. Iskra does not silently rewrite the memo. It marks the
traction claim as disputed, lowers confidence, updates readiness, records the
change in the evidence history, and tells the team exactly what to verify next.
That is the difference between fluent AI and decision-grade AI.”

## 3:40 - Cold Starts And Scenarios

Open a cold-start company, then open Outcomes.

“For a founder with little public data, Iskra marks a cold start and exposes the
missing history instead of pretending the evidence is stronger than it is. Then
the outcome simulator lets the investor change growth, churn, margin, burn, and
dilution to see runway, next-round valuation, ownership, expected return, and
MOIC update immediately.”

## 4:15 - Team And Iskra

Open Comments and then Iskra.

“The decision is a shared workspace. Teammates can anchor comments, mention one
another, assign diligence tasks, and resolve threads. Iskra can search the
portfolio, compare tagged analyses, start a new analysis, or answer questions
about an attached document. Dictation keeps the normal chat interface; Dialogue
adds automatic turn-taking and optional ElevenLabs narration.”

## 4:45 - Close

“Iskra does not claim that AI can know the answer. It gives an investment team a
faster path to an answer while preserving what is known, disputed, and still
missing. That is how a $100,000 decision becomes evidence-backed within 24 hours.”

## Demo Safety Net

The demo runs without paid API keys using synthetic data and deterministic
fallbacks. For a live-key presentation, use a small OpenAI model and one
explicit search provider. Keep enrichment to one result per founder and use
ElevenLabs only for the final dialogue moment. If a live provider is unavailable,
continue with the seeded evidence and explain that the same pipeline remains
available through its fallback path.
