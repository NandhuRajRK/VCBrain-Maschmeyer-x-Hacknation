# AetherGrid Customer Reference Notes

Source type: customer_reference
Collected: 2026-07-18

## Northstar Mobility

Northstar Mobility is a paid customer using AetherGrid for batch inference. The reference contact confirmed 2 production workloads and approximately 450,000 inference requests in the last quarter.

The contact described the product as a promising pilot that is still expanding beyond one team. They did not confirm 20 enterprise customers or 3.1 million quarterly requests across the company.

## Design partners

Three additional companies are listed as design partners. They are not yet paying customers according to the reference notes.

## Verification result

Independent customer evidence supports early production usage, but it does not support the deck's claim of 20 enterprise customers. Request a customer list, signed contracts, and a revenue cohort export before relying on the larger traction claim.
