# AetherGrid Pitch Deck

## Company

AetherGrid is an AI infrastructure company based in Berlin, Germany. Its platform routes inference jobs across available GPU capacity and aims to reduce serving cost and latency for European enterprises.

## Founding team

Lena Vogt is the founder and chief executive. She previously worked on distributed systems at a European cloud provider. The deck describes her as a repeat founder, but does not name the prior company or provide an outcome.

Omar Haddad is the founding engineer. He previously built GPU scheduling systems for a research computing group and maintains public open-source infrastructure projects.

## Product

AetherGrid provides a policy layer for routing inference workloads across cloud and private GPU providers. The product claims to keep p95 inference latency below customer-defined limits while reducing idle GPU capacity.

## Market

The initial buyer is an engineering or infrastructure team operating high-volume inference workloads. The company estimates a serviceable European market of 1.2 billion euros, but the calculation and source are not included in this deck.

## Traction

The deck states that AetherGrid has 20 enterprise customers, 42% month-over-month inference volume growth, and 3.1 million inference requests processed in the last quarter.

It also describes the current customer base as 5 paid customers and 15 design partners. The relationship between these numbers is not explained and should be verified.

## Financials and round

AetherGrid reports 180,000 euros in annual recurring revenue, 42% gross margin, and 14 months of runway. It is raising a 1.5 million euro seed round at a 7.5 million euro pre-money valuation.

## Risks

The company depends on access to scarce GPU capacity and has not supplied signed customer contracts, a cohort view, or an independent market-sizing source.
