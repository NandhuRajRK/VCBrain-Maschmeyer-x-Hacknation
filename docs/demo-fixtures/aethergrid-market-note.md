# Independent Market Note: Inference Infrastructure

Source type: third_party_market_note
Collected: 2026-07-18

European demand for inference infrastructure is growing as companies move production AI workloads out of experimentation. The most relevant buying signals for AetherGrid are workload volume, GPU utilization, latency requirements, cloud concentration, and willingness to use a third-party routing layer.

This note does not verify AetherGrid's claimed 1.2 billion euro serviceable market. A proper diligence pass should request the underlying market definition, customer segment, pricing assumptions, and bottom-up account count.

Competitive risk is meaningful. Cloud providers, GPU marketplaces, and internal platform teams can each build parts of the routing workflow. AetherGrid needs evidence of switching costs, measurable savings, or better reliability.

Open question: does the product improve gross margin and latency for paying customers after cloud and GPU capacity costs are included?
